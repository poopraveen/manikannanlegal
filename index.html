About the Advocate

A Legacy of Trusted
Legal Advocacy

Advocate P. Manikannan is a distinguished legal professional primarily practicing at the High Court of Madras and various civil courts across Chennai, Tamil Nadu. His practice is defined by rigorous legal scholarship, unwavering ethical standards, and a genuine commitment to every client's cause.

With extensive experience across civil, criminal, and service-related matters, he has built a reputation for delivering strategic, result-oriented legal representation. His track record includes successful writ petitions against government bodies, civil revision cases, and complex family court matters.

He is documented in official lawyer profiles on eCourtsIndia and Indian Kanoon, reflecting an active and transparent professional career in Tamil Nadu's judicial ecosystem.
