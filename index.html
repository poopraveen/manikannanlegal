About the Advocate

A Legacy of Trusted
Legal Advocacy

Advocate Poongavanan Manikannan is a distinguished legal professional with over 25 years of practice at the High Court of Madras and civil courts across Chennai, Tamil Nadu. His work is built on rigorous legal knowledge, strong ethics, and a genuine dedication to every client's case.

He handles a wide range of matters — from civil appeals and criminal cases to property documentation, debt recovery, matrimonial disputes, and tribunal proceedings. Whether you face a cheque bounce issue, a property title problem, or a government service grievance, he provides clear and effective legal guidance.

He is documented in official lawyer profiles on eCourtsIndia and Indian Kanoon, reflecting an active and transparent professional career spanning over two decades in Tamil Nadu's judicial ecosystem.

What We Handle

Areas of Legal Practice

Comprehensive legal representation across the full spectrum of civil, criminal, and administrative law in Tamil Nadu.

⚖️

Civil Litigation & Appeals

If someone has filed a case against you — or you need to take someone to court over money, property, or a broken agreement — this covers you. Handles Civil Suits, Appeal Suits (A.S.), Second Appeals (S.A.), Civil Revision Petitions (C.R.P.), and Land Acquisition disputes.

🔒

Criminal Law & Cheque Bounce

Accused of a crime or cheated by someone whose cheque bounced? Get strong defence or pursue the offender in court. Covers all criminal matters, bail applications, quashing petitions, and Cheque Bounce cases under Section 138 of the Negotiable Instruments Act.

Enquire Now →
👨‍👩‍👧

Matrimonial & Family Law

Going through a difficult family situation? Get compassionate and confidential legal support. Handles divorce, maintenance (monthly support payments), child custody disputes, matrimonial revision petitions, and all related family court matters across Tamil Nadu.

Enquire Now →
🏛️

Labour, Service & Employment

Wrongfully dismissed, denied a promotion, or facing a service dispute with a government employer? We fight for your rights. Covers labour disputes, industrial matters, service law litigations, and cases before the Central Administrative Tribunal (CAT) and HR & CE Department matters.

Enquire Now →
📝

Legal Documentation & Property

Need any legal document drafted, verified, or a property checked before you sign? Every document is prepared carefully to protect your interests.

Documents: Sale Deed, Partition Deed, Index Correction (MOD), Deposit of Title Deeds, Declaration Deed, Affidavit, Will, Indemnity Bond, Construction Agreement, Rental Agreement.

Property & POA: Title Verification, Legal Opinion on Property, General Power of Attorney (GPA), Special Power of Attorney (SPA), Cancellation of GPA.

Enquire Now →
🏦

Debt Recovery & Banking (DRT / SARFAESI)

Bank threatening to seize your property over a loan? Or are you a lender trying to recover money? We handle cases at the Debt Recovery Tribunal (DRT), Debt Recovery Appellate Tribunal (DRAT), SARFAESI Act proceedings, and Rent Control Cases.

Enquire Now →
🚆

Tribunals & Accident Cases

Injured in an accident or have a dispute before a special tribunal? Claim the compensation you deserve. Handles Motor Accident Compensation cases (MCOP Act), Railway Claims Tribunal, Green Tribunal (environmental disputes), and Senior Citizen Protection Act matters.

Enquire Now →
📜

Writ, Probate & Original Side

Need to challenge a government order in the High Court, or settle a deceased person's estate? We handle High Court Writ Petitions (against unlawful government actions), Original Side matters, Probate of Will, Letters of Administration, and Gazette Publications for name or document corrections.

Enquire Now →
🏢

Insolvency

Unable to repay debts or dealing with a bankrupt individual or company? We guide you through the entire process. Handles personal and corporate insolvency matters, creditor claims, debt restructuring proceedings, and representation before the National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT) under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC).

Enquire Now →
🤝

Arbitration

Want to resolve a dispute quickly and privately — without going through a lengthy court process? Arbitration is a faster, confidential alternative. We handle commercial, contractual, and property disputes through arbitration, including drafting arbitration clauses, representing clients before arbitrators, and enforcing arbitral awards in court.
